The Best Movies According to Vanessa

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Hero (2002)

I love this film for it's artistry, color, visual settings, fluid movement and story telling.

The Matrix

This sci-fi movie was ground breaking for me. The movement, the style, the music, the drama and action.

The Handmaiden (2016)

A South Korean historical psychological thriller that is intriguing, provoking and sexy.
